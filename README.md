## üéØ How It Works

1. **Authenticate**: Log in with your Spotify account via OAuth 2.0
2. **Data Collection**: App retrieves your top artists, tracks, and listening analytics
3. **AI Generation**: ChatGPT creates personalized trivia questions based on your unique music taste
4. **Play & Learn**: Answer questions about your own listening habits and discover hidden insights
5. **Analytics Dashboard**: View listening data not normally visible in Spotify's interface
